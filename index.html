Your Role and Responsibilities

IBM Z is the world's most powerful business platform, hosting a significant portion of enterprise workloads, data, and transactions. We are on a transformative journey to enhance the developer experience on IBM Z by embracing disruptive technologies across development, testing, deployment, and monitoring.

To achieve this transformation, we are hiring top talent for various positions, including offering management, design, and development. Join us on this thrilling journey, leverage cutting-edge technologies at work, and deliver meaningful innovation for the platform that drives businesses.

The Position: Full Stack Developer

We are seeking exceptional Full Stack software development talent to support new projects that pave the way for Cognitive Operations in Z Systems Software. Our goal is to build dynamic teams that collaborate with industry leaders to develop an advanced approach to IT operations analytics.

As a Full Stack developer, you will be an integral part of a highly focused, self-managed team responsible for designing, developing, and testing cognitive insights into Z Systems workloads and applications. Working closely with Designers, you will apply IBM Design Thinking methodology in software design, gathering requirements from stakeholders and Sponsor Users.

Who You Are

  • Thrive on finding and resolving complex technical issues.
  • Possess a natural curiosity and creativity, coupled with a passion for technology.
  • Value teamwork and lead by example.
  • Embrace empathy in all interactions with clients, teams, designs, and tasks.
  • Demonstrate interest in, understanding of, or experience with Design Thinking.
  • Possess strong verbal and written communication skills, as well as technical prowess.
  • Have experience with Agile Development and AI concepts.

Required Technical and Professional Expertise

  • At least 5+ years of experience in software development.
  • At least 3 - 5 years of experience in Web technologies such as JavaScript frameworks (Angular2, React, Node, bootstrap), HTML5, CSS3.
  • At least 3 - 5 years of experience in Java and REST API development.
  • Strong experience working with NoSQL technologies.
  • Knowledge of and experience in machine learning methodologies.
  • Knowledge of and experience in implementing AI solutions.
  • 5 years of experience in Agile software development methodologies.
  • Basic z/OS administrative knowledge.
  • VS code IDE foundation (Editor, Lang, Debug, Explorer, Plugin extensions).

Essential Requirements

  • Experience building and shipping front-end applications written in TypeScript/JavaScript (TypeScript preferred).
  • Experience building user experiences with ReactJs.
  • Proficiency with modern development tools and methods such as VS Code, Git, GitHub/GitLab, Travis, Behavior-Driven Development, Scrum, SAFE, or equivalent.

Preferred Technical and Professional Expertise

  • 5 years of experience in z/OS software development.
  • Knowledge of and experience in data science work in support of cognitive solutions.
  • Proficiency with VS Code and expertise in automated building and packaging Nodejs applications.
  • Experience building and shipping VS Code extensions.
  • Experience building Java enterprise applications.
